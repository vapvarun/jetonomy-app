# Jetonomy App — Documentation Hub

Welcome! The Jetonomy App turns your [Jetonomy](https://store.wbcomdesigns.com/jetonomy/) community into a native iPhone and Android app — no app-development experience required.

Your community lives on your own WordPress site. The app connects directly to it. No third-party servers, no separate account, no monthly middleman.

---

## What is the Jetonomy App?

It's an open-source mobile app (React Native / Expo, GPL-2.0) built and maintained by [Wbcom Designs](https://store.wbcomdesigns.com/jetonomy/). Source code: [github.com/vapvarun/jetonomy-app](https://github.com/vapvarun/jetonomy-app).

Members use the app to read and post in your community spaces, vote, reply, search, and get notifications — all in real time, straight from their phone.

Admins and moderators get a dedicated **Manage** area: moderation queue, flags, announcements, and analytics.

---

## How it works (the short version)

```
Member's phone  ──►  Jetonomy App  ──►  Your WordPress site (jetonomy/v1 REST API)
```

No central Jetonomy server sits in the middle. The app talks directly to your site using WordPress's built-in Application Passwords. You own the data.
